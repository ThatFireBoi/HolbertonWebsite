FROM MY HANDS


During my time at Full Sail, specifically the last five months, I created something I'm very proud of and never expected to actually be a part of: with a team of five, including myself, we created a game called Nearline. While I had made small prototypes and bite-sized games before, this was the first time that it was being made from scratch: pre-production, making a desing Bible, prototypes, production, creating every single model, sound, texture, piece of code, etc. It was a true labor of love, one involving countless sleepless nights, toiling away amidst the glow of the computer screen.

As a result of this endeavor, we started a company called Studio 68, with the intention of one day developing the game further, put into it the knowledge we have acquired throughout the years, and eventually, release it to the public and seeing what happens. Despite fairly busy lives, it's something we still talk about and work towards when time permits.
